## 🔍 Overview

<a href="#fourUKey">4uKey</a> serves iPhone and iPad owners who have genuinely lost access to their own devices through forgotten passcodes, devices inherited without access credentials, or organizational restrictions on purchased used devices — providing a recovery path when Apple's standard account recovery options aren't available or applicable.

The <a href="#fourUKey">forgotten passcode use case</a> is the most common application — a long-time device owner who has forgotten an infrequently-used passcode, or who set up a complex passcode that was subsequently lost, needs to regain access to a personally-owned device with no other recovery option readily available. The <a href="#fourUKey">used device purchase scenario</a> applies when a legitimately purchased used iPhone or iPad retains a previous owner's Apple ID or organizational MDM restrictions that the previous owner didn't remove before sale — 4uKey provides a path to clear these restrictions for legitimate new ownership.

The <a href="#fourUKey">parental Screen Time management</a> use case addresses parents who set a Screen Time passcode for managing a child's device and subsequently forgot it — needing to regain administrative control over Screen Time settings without performing a full device reset that would lose the child's data and settings. <a href="#fourUKey">IT and device management scenarios</a> apply 4uKey for organizations managing fleets of devices where MDM removal is needed for legitimately decommissioned devices being repurposed or resold.</p>
